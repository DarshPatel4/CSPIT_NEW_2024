ELECTRONICS DEVICES AND CIRCUITS (EDC)


The laboratory is designed to give exposure in practical works for each and every concept, theory, and phenomena’s in the analog world of Electronics starting from basic of diode, transistor, operational amplifier, filters & oscillators. The laboratory occupied for practical works of the subjects like Integrated Circuits and Applications (ICA), Project Work, Solid State Electronics (SSE). Etc. The laboratory is equipped with the main equipment’s like Digital/Analog Storage DSO/CRO 100MHz, Freq. Counter, CRO with DMM, Freq. Counter, Logic-Analyzer, Complete PCB Designing Solution PROTEL DXP PCB Designing software.
