High Voltage Engineering Lab


High Voltage Engineering Lab resides at lab No. 116B, ground floor EE building. This lab is well equipped with BDV oil test kit, Oil dissipation factor and dc resisitivity measurement kit, cascade transformer of 100 kVAC, 140 kV DC test set up, 150 kV, 1.5 kJ Impulse Voltage Generator, 30-0-30 kV Insulation Tester, DSO, Sphere Gap and Rod Gap with different geometries for high voltage measurement and investigation, Insulator test bench, Electrolytic Tank etc. Students can understand/perform the experiments to learn and investigate the performance of various insulations in different environmental conditions under HVDC, HVAC and Short duration Impulse voltages.

Course Information

Equipments
High Voltage Engineering Test Bench for flashover studies of insulator, Oil BDV Test kit, Dissipation Factor and DC resistivity meter, 150 kV impulse voltage generator, 100kV AC/140kV DC high voltage tester, 30-0-30 kV insulation tester, Electrolytic tank, Horn gap apparatus

Projects


  • Evaluated Ester Oil Characteristics,Hot Spot and Overloading Capacity Analysis with Temperature Rise Test on Ester Oil filled Power Transformers
  • Conducted Flashover probability studies over the line insulators under various contamination possessions
  • Incepient fault diagnosis of mineral oil using Fuzzy logic approach
  • Simulation based studies of nanoparticle's Influence On Dielectric Properties Of Transformer Oil Based Nanofluids
  • Finite Element Based Electric Field and Electric Potential Distribution Analysis of Insulators
  • Influence of Polarity on Breakdown Characteristics of Insulators
  • Implementation of existing 150 kV Impulse Voltage Generator as an Impulse Current Generator.
