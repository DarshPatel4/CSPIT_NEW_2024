Power Electronic and Drives/Network Analysis Lab


Power Electronic and Drives / Network Analysis resides at lab No. 117C, ground floor EE building. This lab is well equipped with inbuilt DC/AC supply breadboards. Various kits of basic operational characteristics of SCR , DIAC, TRIAC , UJT , Bridge rectifiers and Inverters are avilable for the better understanding of the each power devices. Student can make the hardware project in the lab and they also can use other facilitoes like Digital Storage Oscillopscope (DSO) , Multisignal Storage Oscillosope (MSO) , Power analyser.
